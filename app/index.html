About Me

Hi! My name is Mariana. I'm a fan of the Seattle Seahawks American football team. My favorite player is Richard Sherman. I like to read adventure books, to build LEGO, to listen a good Samba (Brazilian musical genre), to write for my Facebook page. Also, I love Lord of the Rings’ universe.

I graduated in History, specialized in Brazilian History. During the bachelor, I realized that the impact of my work in society didn't satisfy me. So, I decided to try something completely different and I don’t regret about it. Almost 24 years old, I discovered that my passion for front-end development could be my profession in the future. Although I still enjoy reading about Brazilian History, currently I’m in love with the front-end development.
